Competitions

Last year, we attended the State Leadership Conference. We competed in a lot of events considering our chapter size, like CO2 car, Technology Problem Solving, CAD, On Demand Video, Biotechnology Design, and more. We had a few students make nationals as well. This year, with a VEX Robotics team, for the first time, we attended the Fall Leadership Conference this year, where we competed in the Armada Boat Race and VEX. This year, we have grown in attendees for the State Leadership Conference and we are competing in even more events than last year, including VEX Robotics. We also intend to compete in electric car for the first time.
